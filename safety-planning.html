Safety Planning Guide

Empower yourself with a personalized safety plan that prepares you for emergencies and helps ensure your safety.

Why a Safety Plan?

A safety plan is a practical tool designed to help you stay safe in different situations, whether you are at home, work, or out in public. It helps you make prepared decisions, access resources, and reduce risk in times of crisis.

Steps to Create Your Safety Plan

  1. Identify Safe Places: Think of safe locations where you can go during an emergency, such as a trusted friendâ€™s house, a shelter, or a community center.
  2. Prepare Important Contacts: Make a list of emergency contacts, including local law enforcement, a nearby hospital, and trusted individuals you can rely on.
  3. Keep Essentials Ready: Have a small bag with essentials like identification, medications, emergency cash, and spare keys. Keep it in a safe, accessible place.
  4. Practice Safety in Public: Be mindful of your surroundings, plan your route, and keep your phone charged when going out.
  5. Know Your Resources: Familiarize yourself with local support services, such as hotlines, shelters, and support groups that offer assistance.
