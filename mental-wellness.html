Program Overview

The Mental Wellness Program aims to provide a safe space for women to focus on their mental health. This program includes counseling, stress-relief techniques, mindfulness practices, and emotional support to help women lead balanced and healthy lives.

Key Benefits

  • Access to licensed mental health counselors for personal support
  • Stress management workshops and relaxation techniques
  • Mindfulness and meditation sessions to improve emotional resilience
  • A supportive community environment to share experiences and advice

How It Works

The program includes individual counseling sessions, group workshops, and weekly mindfulness sessions. Participants can choose from in-person or virtual meetings depending on their location and preferences. In addition, we provide online resources and tools for ongoing support.
