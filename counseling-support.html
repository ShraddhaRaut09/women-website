Counseling Support

Access professional counseling services and find support for emotional and mental wellness.

Professional Counseling Services

Explore licensed counseling services to help you navigate emotional challenges, stress, and mental health needs.

  • BetterHelp: BetterHelp offers online therapy with licensed counselors.
  • Talkspace: Talkspace provides affordable and convenient therapy sessions online.
  • 7 Cups: 7 Cups offers emotional support and free anonymous conversations with trained listeners.

Mental Health Hotlines

If you need immediate support, these hotlines offer 24/7 assistance from trained professionals.

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 1-800-273-8255
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741 for free, confidential support.
  • Substance Abuse Helpline: 1-800-662-4357

Online Support Groups

Connect with others who share similar experiences in these support groups, providing mutual understanding and encouragement.

  • Support Groups Central: Support Groups Central provides free and affordable group support sessions.
  • Daily Strength: Daily Strength is an online community for support across various mental health and wellness topics.
  • SMART Recovery: SMART Recovery offers support groups for addiction recovery.
