Breathing Exercises

Try these simple breathing techniques to manage stress and bring yourself to a calmer state.

  • Deep Breathing: Inhale slowly through your nose, hold for a few seconds, then exhale through your mouth.
  • 4-7-8 Breathing: Breathe in for 4 seconds, hold for 7, and exhale for 8. Repeat to relax the body.
  • Box Breathing: Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4, exhale for 4, and hold again for 4. Repeat the cycle.
