Financial Assistance

Explore financial aid options, grants, and resources to support your journey toward stability and growth.

Government Assistance Programs

Government programs can offer vital support. Here are some options that may be available to you:

  • Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP): Provides funds for food for eligible individuals and families.
  • Temporary Assistance for Needy Families (TANF): Financial aid for low-income families with children to cover basic needs.
  • Housing Assistance: Offers affordable housing options and rental support through various programs.

Emergency Financial Aid

Organizations that provide emergency funds to help with unexpected expenses and crises:

  • Modest Needs: A charity that offers short-term financial aid to help with unexpected financial needs.
  • Crisis Assistance Ministry: Provides emergency financial assistance for rent, utilities, and more.
  • United Way 211: Connects individuals with local resources for financial support in times of need.
